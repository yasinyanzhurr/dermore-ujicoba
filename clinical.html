Human-Based Clinical Study

Evaluating real skin response through science-backed observation.

Clinical Evaluation Overview

Dermore Bioactive Serum was developed with a science-first formulation approach and evaluated through a human-based observational clinical study.

The study focused on skin hydration, comfort, appearance, and overall tolerability during consistent daily use.

Study Design

The evaluation was conducted over a 4-week period involving female participants aged 20–45 with various skin types, including sensitive and acne-prone skin.

Participants applied Dermore Bioactive Serum twice daily, morning and evening, on clean skin, without introducing additional active treatments.

  • Skin hydration and moisture comfort
  • Skin smoothness and texture appearance
  • Skin brightness and overall glow
  • Skin tolerance and irritation response
  • Overall skin condition perception

Clinical Results Summary

After four weeks of consistent use, the majority of participants experienced improved skin hydration and overall skin comfort.

Skin appeared healthier, smoother, and more radiant, with high tolerability observed across different skin types.

Safety & Skin Compatibility

Throughout the evaluation period, Dermore Bioactive Serum demonstrated high skin compatibility.

No severe irritation or adverse skin reactions were reported, supporting its suitability for daily use, including for sensitive and acne-prone skin.

Results may vary depending on individual skin condition and usage consistency. This clinical evaluation supports formulation performance and does not replace professional dermatological treatment.
