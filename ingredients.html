The Science Behind Dermoree

Where Science Meets Nature

Dermoree is formulated through a science-first lens guided by contemporary skin biology, regenerative research, and a deep respect for naturally occurring bioactive systems.

Rather than following trends, we study how different compounds interact with the skin’s environment and select those that complement its natural balance.

Each ingredient is chosen for its functional role in supporting skin condition, resilience, and long-term comfort.

A Thoughtful Combination

Dermoree’s formulation is built on purposeful synergy. Each component is selected not for immediate effect, but for how it contributes to overall skin condition when used consistently over time.

Nothing excessive. Nothing impulsive. Only what is relevant, intentional, and beneficial to the skin.

Centella Exosome (Japan)

An advanced bioactive delivery system derived from Centella asiatica, reflecting a modern approach to ingredient delivery inspired by Japanese skin science.

Through next-generation exosome technology, the essence of Centella is designed to support the deeper skin environment, helping maintain comfort, balance, and resilience over time.

This technology supports more efficient interaction within the skin environment, contributing to calm, barrier refinement, and well-conditioned skin.

Salmon DNA (PDRN-Inspired)

Inspired by regenerative dermatology, Salmon DNA supports skin renewal and recovery.

With consistent use, it helps improve skin smoothness, resilience, and a healthier-looking appearance.

Fermented Honey

Through fermentation, honey becomes enriched with naturally occuring probiotics that support a healthy balanced skin microbiome.

A balanced microbiome is essential for maintaining calm, hydrated, and resilient skin.

Niacinamide

A clinically recognized ingredient known for its ability to support skin brightening, barrier repair, and overall skin balance.

Niacinamide helps improve uneven skin tone while strengthening the skin’s natural defense.
