Where Science Meets Nature

Dermoree is formulated through a science-first lens guided by contemporary skin biology, regenerative research, and a deep respect for naturally occurring bioactive systems.

Rather than following trends, we study how different compounds interact with the skin’s environment and select those that complement its natural balance.

Each ingredient is chosen for its functional role in supporting skin condition, resilience, and long-term comfort.

A Thoughtful Combination

Dermoree’s formulation is built on purposeful synergy. Each component is selected not for immediate effect, but for how it contributes to overall skin condition when used consistently over time.

Nothing excessive. Nothing impulsive. Only what is relevant, intentional, and beneficial to the skin.

Centella Exosome (Japan)

An advanced bioactive delivery system derived from Centella asiatica, reflecting a modern approach to ingredient delivery inspired by Japanese skin science.

Through next-generation exosome technology, the essence of Centella is designed to support the deeper skin environment, helping maintain comfort, balance, and resilience over time.

This technology supports more efficient interaction within the skin environment, contributing to calm, barrier refinement, and well-conditioned skin.

Salmon DNA (PDRN-Inspired)

Inspired by regenerative dermatology, Salmon DNA is a bioactive component recognized for its role in supporting skin renewal and recovery at a structural level.

Drawing from PDRN-inspired research, this ingredient helps encourage skin resilience by supporting the natural repair environment within the skin.

With consistent use, Salmon DNA contributes to improved skin smoothness, enhanced elasticity, and a healthier-looking appearance over time.

Rather than offering instant cosmetic correction, it supports gradual skin improvement aligned with the skin’s natural renewal cycle.

Fermented Honey

Through a natural fermentation process, honey becomes enriched with bioavailable compounds that support a healthy and balanced skin microbiome.

Fermentation enhances the functional properties of honey, allowing beneficial components to interact more effectively with the skin environment.

A well-supported microbiome plays a crucial role in maintaining skin comfort, hydration balance, and barrier integrity.

By helping preserve microbial harmony on the skin surface, fermented honey contributes to a calmer, more resilient, and well-conditioned appearance over time.

Niacinamide

Niacinamide is a clinically recognized multifunctional ingredient valued for its role in supporting skin brightening, barrier repair, and overall skin balance.

By strengthening the skin’s natural defense system, niacinamide helps improve resilience against environmental stressors.

With consistent use, it contributes to a more even skin tone while supporting a smoother and healthier-looking complexion.

Niacinamide also plays a role in maintaining optimal hydration levels by reinforcing the skin barrier’s ability to retain moisture.
