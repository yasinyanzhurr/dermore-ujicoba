The Science Behind Dermore

Advanced bioactive ingredients inspired by Japanese innovation and natural biodiversity.

Where Science Meets Nature

Dermore is formulated with a science-first approach, combining modern skin biology with carefully selected natural bioactive compounds.

Each ingredient is chosen not for trends, but for its role in supporting long-term skin health, balance, and resilience.

Centella Exosome (Japan)

An advanced bioactive delivery system derived from Centella Asiatica, known for its regenerative and anti-inflammatory properties.

Exosome technology supports more efficient skin communication, helping calm irritation and support skin recovery at a cellular level.

Salmon DNA (PDRN-Inspired)

Inspired by regenerative dermatology, Salmon DNA supports skin renewal and recovery.

This ingredient helps improve skin texture, resilience, and overall skin condition with consistent use.

Fermented Honey

Through fermentation, honey becomes rich in probiotics that support a healthy skin microbiome.

A balanced microbiome is essential for maintaining calm, hydrated, and resilient skin.

Niacinamide

A clinically recognized ingredient known for its ability to support skin brightening, barrier repair, and overall skin balance.

Niacinamide helps improve uneven skin tone while strengthening the skin’s natural defense.

Formulation Philosophy

Dermore follows a minimalist formulation philosophy: only what the skin needs, nothing unnecessary.

Every ingredient serves a clear function, working synergistically to support healthy, balanced skin over time.

Dermore is built on research, intention, and respect for the skin’s natural process.
